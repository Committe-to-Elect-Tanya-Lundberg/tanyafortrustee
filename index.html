Hello from Tanya

Hi there! I’m Tanya Lundberg and I am running for Northville Township Trustee.

My husband, Jeff, and I moved to Northville Township in 2009, just before our son was born. We wanted to find a place where we could plant roots, raise our son, and someday retire. When we walked into our house with our realtor, it was like a movie – we knew we had found our family’s home. We’ve enjoyed living here ever since and are invested in ensuring that Northville is a thriving place for all residents and visitors.

As an attorney and leader in the metro Detroit legal community, I’ve developed important skills that will make me an effective Township Trustee, including analytical, negotiation, problem-solving, research, and relationship-building skills. I’m running for the opportunity to put these skills to work for our community and be the voice that’s been missing from our Township Board.

Thank you for visiting my website and I hope I earn your vote.

Tanya
